Components

Here we show the components of our project !

ARDUINO MEGA 2560

It's the brain, because it contains a Microprocessor so we can manipulate data, memory and input/Output.

H-BRIDGE L298N

It's the middleman - Takes 12v from the battary, then gives the Arduino Chip the 5v it needs - gives the servo motors of wheels the 9v they need.

Bluetooth Module HC-05

It transfers data to the Arduino Chip using any device support bluetooth with a simple application to control it.

Battery DC 12300 12V

Used for Supplying The required voltage for H-bridge, which in turn supplies the Arduino and the car's motors.

Two DC Motors

Used to give the wheels the ability to spin therefore the car can move.

Three Wheels

Two of them are controlled using motors & the other one is to facilitate the motion.

Wires

The buses that compact all the components.

A Device that supports bluetooth, like mobile phone

It's used to give the orders, thus the car move from one place to another.
